About KAARI

KAARI was born from the dreams of three women who share a deep passion for designing spaces that are not just beautiful, but truly meaningful. The name “KAARI” comes from Sanskrit, a language as timeless as the principles of design we hold close to our hearts. It embodies our commitment to creating spaces that feel warm, inviting, and full of life.

To us, interior design is far more than arranging furniture or choosing colors—it’s about shaping environments that tell stories, evoke emotions, and leave people feeling fulfilled. KAARI is a reflection of who we are—rooted in tradition, driven by creativity, and dedicated to crafting spaces that connect people to their surroundings in the most genuine and heartfelt way.
